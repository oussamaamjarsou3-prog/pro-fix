🔍 Mega Menu Anatomy

1
Trigger (Nav Item)

The link/button you hover or click to open the menu

2
Dropdown Panel

The large panel that appears — can be full-width or contained

3
Columns / Groups

Content organized in columns, categories, or grid cards

4
Featured / Promo

Optional rich content: image cards, CTAs, banners

5
Close Trigger

Clicking outside or moving mouse away closes the menu

6
Animation

Smooth fade + slide to make it feel premium & polished
